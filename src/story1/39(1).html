I Feel Happy

I feel happy… because for once, someone actually listened. You didn't cut me off, you didn't try to fix me, and you didn't make me feel like I was too much. That doesn't happen often. Even though I came off as angry or defensive, deep down, I just wanted someone to see that I was struggling — that I felt like I was losing control of everything around me. And when I lashed out, it wasnt because I hated you… it was because I was scared. Tired. Frustrated. Hurt. But you stayed. You listened. You didn't take it personally and that made me feel safe, even if just for a moment. Like maybe I'm not as alone as I thought.
