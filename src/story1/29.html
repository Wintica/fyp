Recommended Response

  • Preserve dignity - Never speak to her like a child
  • Offer choices - Give her control over decisions when possible
  • Use collaborative language - "Let's..." instead of "You need to..."
  • Validate emotions - Acknowledge her feelings even if behavior seems irrational
  • Stay calm - Your emotional state directly affects hers
  • Focus on abilities - Emphasize what she can do, not what she can't
  • Allow extra time - Rush creates anxiety and resistance
