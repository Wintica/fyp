I Feel Defeated

I feel defeated… because once again, nobody really heard me. You talked over me, you tried to fix me like I was broken, and you made me feel like I was just being difficult. This happens all the time. Even though I tried to explain myself, you just saw the anger on the surface. You didn't see that I was struggling, that I felt like everything was slipping away from me. And when I got upset, you took it personally and got defensive right back. That made me feel like I was the problem. But you left — emotionally, even if you stayed physically. You stopped listening. You made it about how hard this was for you, and that made me feel more alone than ever. Like I really am just a burden that everyone has to endure.
